2. Why Have a Home Stock Inventory System?

If you’re asking about tracking household goods, reasons include:

	Emergency Preparedness – Know what supplies (food, medicine, etc.) you have.
	Prevent Overstocking/Waste – Avoid buying duplicates or letting things expire.
	Insurance Purposes – Helps in case of loss (fire, theft, disaster).
	Budgeting – Track spending on household goods.


Overcomes

1. Clear idea about spending stock and restocking.
2. Manage products with they are own budget.
3. Documentation for the insurance purposes.
4. Reducing wastage and fully efficiency.
